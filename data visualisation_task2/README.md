# Sales Dashboard - README

## Overview

This README provides an overview and usage guide for the **Sales Dashboard**. The dashboard visualizes key sales metrics, enabling users to track performance across regions, product categories, and time periods. It helps stakeholders identify trends, measure achievement against targets, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Features

### **1. KPI Indicators**

* **Total Sales** – Displays overall revenue.
* **Total Profit** – Shows profit generated during the selected date period.
* **Achievement (%)** – Measures performance against the sales target.
* **Sales vs Target** – Compares actual sales to the target value.

### **2. Interactive Filters**

* **Product Category Filter** (Electronics, Furniture, Office Supplies)
* **Region Filter** (East, West, North, South)
* **Order Date Range Filter** with slider and calendar input
* **Year Selection** (2019, 2020)

These filters allow users to drill down and customize their analysis.

---

## Visualizations Included

### **1. Sales vs Target Over Time**

Shows how actual sales compare with the target, including percentage difference.

### **2. Sum of Sales by Region**

Highlights the performance of each region, helping identify strong and weak areas.

### **3. Sum of Sales by Product Category**

Breaks down revenue by category, showing contributions and percentage shares.

### **4. Sales by Month and Year**

Line chart showing monthly performance for different years, useful for seasonality analysis.

---

## Key Insights from Dashboard

* East region has the highest sales performance.
* Electronics is the top-performing product category.
* Achievement rate is above 100%, indicating targets are being met.
* Sales peak towards the end of the year (October–December).

---

## How to Use the Dashboard

1. Open the Power BI file (.pbix) or the published dashboard.
2. Use filters at the top to select region, category, or date range.
3. Hover over graphs to see detailed tooltips.
4. Compare KPIs to evaluate overall performance.
5. Analyze trends to support business decision-making.
